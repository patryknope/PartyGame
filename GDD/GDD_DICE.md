# GDD_DICE

## Overview
Dice are separate from Items.

## Dice Collection
- Unlimited collection
- No Dice Slots
- No Dice Loadouts
- Select Dice before movement

## Dice Acquisition
- Shop
- Production Building rewards
- Board rewards
- Casino rewards
- Events

## Accepted Dice
Basic: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
Safe: 3 3 4 4 5 5 6 6
Travel: 2 2 4 4 8 8 10 10
Risk: 0 0 0 0 10 10 10 10
Jackpot: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 Jackpot Bust

## Jackpot Design Notes
Mostly small rewards.
Rare medium rewards.
Very rare major rewards.
Rare VIP access rewards.

No teleports.

## Bust Philosophy
Dangerous but not game-ending.
