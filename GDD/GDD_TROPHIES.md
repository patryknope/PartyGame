# GDD_TROPHIES

## Overview
Trophies are the primary win condition of PartyGame.
The player with the most Trophies at the end of the match wins.
All Trophies have equal value — no Trophy is worth more than another.

## Main Trophy
Only one Main Trophy exists at a time.
When collected it respawns elsewhere on the board.
Respawn location is chosen by the board.

Purpose:
- Keep players moving
- Create map interaction
- Generate player conflict

## Board Trophies
Board Trophies are unique to each board and connected to its core mechanic.

Casino:
- Main Event Trophy
- Mini Event Trophy
- Special Room Trophy

Future boards will have their own themed variants.

## Bonus Trophies
Awarded based on player performance at the end of the match.

Default setup:
- 3 Success Trophies
- 3 Neutral Trophies
- 3 Comeback Trophies

Total: 9 Bonus Trophies

## Trophy Customization
Players may configure Bonus Trophy settings in the lobby.
- Enable/Disable categories
- Change Trophy count
- Future custom rules

## Trophy Equality
Every Trophy grants exactly 1 Victory Point.
There are no double-value trophies.
There are no legendary trophies.

## Trophy Respawning
Board-related Trophies may respawn after collection.
Respawn behavior depends on the board.

Purpose:
- Keep players moving
- Prevent static gameplay
- Create repeated opportunities throughout the match

## Canon Decisions
- Trophies are the primary win condition.
- Every Trophy has equal value (1 Victory Point).
- Only one Main Trophy exists at a time.
- Bonus Trophies are configurable in the lobby.
- Board Trophies are board specific.
