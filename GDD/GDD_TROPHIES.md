# GDD_TROPHIES

## Overview
Trophies are the primary win condition.

## Main Trophy
- Only one Main Trophy exists at a time.
- Respawns elsewhere after collection.

Purpose:
- Constant movement
- Map interaction
- Player conflict

## Board Trophies

## Trophy Customization

## Trophy Equality
Every Trophy grants 1 Victory Point.

No:

- Legendary trophies

## Trophy Respawning
Board trophies may respawn depending on board rules.

## Canon Decisions
