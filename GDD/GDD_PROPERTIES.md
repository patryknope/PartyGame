# GDD_PROPERTIES

## Overview
This document covers board structure, tile types and the Property system.
Properties are separate from Buildings — owning a Property provides strategic value and board presence.

## Board Philosophy
Boards should not be simple loops.
Every board should contain multiple branching paths that encourage:
- Route planning
- Risk versus reward decisions
- Player interaction
- Strategic movement

## Route Structure
- Main routes
- Branch routes
- Reconnecting paths

Branches should regularly reconnect to maintain map flow.
Players should never feel trapped in one section of the board.

## Movement
Players move according to their dice result.
Movement continues until all movement points are consumed.
Certain board mechanics may interrupt movement.

## Tile Categories
- Property Tiles
- Reward Tiles
- Penalty Tiles
- Event Tiles
- Shop Tiles
- Board Mechanic Tiles
- Special Room Tiles

Additional tile types may be added in the future.

## Pass-Through Mechanics
Some locations activate when a player passes through them, not only when they stop.
Examples:
- Shops
- VIP Entrances
- Special Board Mechanics

Movement continues after the interaction unless otherwise specified.

## End-Of-Movement Mechanics
Most standard tiles activate only when a player finishes movement on them.
Examples:
- Property Tiles
- Reward Tiles
- Penalty Tiles
- Event Tiles

## Property System
Properties represent ownership of specific board locations.
Properties are separate from Buildings.
Owning a Property provides strategic value and may interact with future systems.

## Property Purchase
When a player finishes movement on an unowned Property Tile, they may purchase it.
Exact purchase cost: OPEN QUESTION

## Property Takeover
Players may take ownership of an unbuilt Property from another player.

Takeover cost: 10 Coins
- 5 Coins to the previous owner
- 5 Coins to the bank

Buildings prevent Property takeovers.
Only the Property ownership changes — the Building stays.

## Influence Zone Restrictions
Control Building Influence Zones are treated as occupied territory.
New Buildings cannot be constructed inside an Influence Zone.
Existing Buildings inside a zone are not affected.

## Canon Decisions
- Boards contain branching routes that reconnect.
- Properties and Buildings are separate systems.
- Players can purchase unowned Properties.
- Players can take over unbuilt Properties for 10 Coins.
- Buildings prevent Property takeovers.
- Influence Zones block future construction inside them.
