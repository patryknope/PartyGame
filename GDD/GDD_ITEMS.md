# GDD_ITEMS

## Overview
Items are consumable tools that give players tactical advantages during a match.
They are divided into two categories: Utility (strategic) and Warfare (aggressive).
Item System details are pending — this document will expand significantly.

## Item Categories

### Utility Items
Strategic items focused on helping the player directly.
- Mobility
- Economy
- Planning
- Board interaction

### Warfare Items
Aggressive items focused on disrupting opponents.
- Sabotage
- Theft
- Combat
- Disruption

## Inventory
Each player has 8 Item Slots.

## Shop Rules
Shop inventory is randomized per player each visit.

Guaranteed minimum per shop visit:
- At least 1 Utility Item
- At least 1 Warfare Item

This ensures players always have a meaningful choice between strategic and aggressive options.

## Canon Decisions
- Players have 8 Item Slots.
- Shop inventory is randomized per player.
- Shop always offers at least 1 Utility and 1 Warfare Item.
