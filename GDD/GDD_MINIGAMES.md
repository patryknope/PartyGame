# GDD_MINIGAMES

## Overview
Minigames occur at the end of every round and provide Coins to players.
They are a primary source of income and a key driver of player interaction.
Early Access target: 25-30 minigames.

## Status
Minigame System is pending full design.

Future work:
- Minigame categories
- Full minigame roster
- Reward structure
- Player count variations

## Open Questions
