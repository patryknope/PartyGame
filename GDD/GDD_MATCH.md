# GDD_MATCH

## Supported Players
2-6

## Match Length
Default: 15 turns

Planned:
- 10
- 15
- 20
- 30

## Match Flow
1. All player turns
2. Minigame
3. Board Mechanic Update
4. Next Round

## Turn Flow
1. Use War Item
2. Select Dice
3. Roll Dice
4. Movement
5. Route Decisions
6. Board Interactions
7. Final Tile Resolution
8. Use Utility Items
9. End Turn

## War Items
Used before movement.

Purpose:
- attacking players
- disruption
- aggression

## Utility Items
Used during turn.

Purpose:
- mobility
- economy
- utility
- planning

## Tie Breakers
Wealth determines winner when Trophy counts are equal.

## Accepted Decisions
- Default match length is 15 turns.
- Wealth breaks ties.
- Utility items are usable during the turn.
- War items are usable before movement.
