\# CHANGELOG



\## 2026-06-12



\### ACCEPTED



Buildings are permanent.



Reason:

Building placement should be a meaningful strategic decision.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Builder is used only for upgrades.



Reason:

Reduce unnecessary gameplay friction.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



One Building per category per player.



Categories:



\* Economic

\* Production

\* Control



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Production Buildings use Courier Delivery.



Additional Rule:



Production is blocked until the current delivery is completed.



Status:

Playtest Required.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Control Buildings create Influence Zones.



Influence Zones block future building construction.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Every Trophy has equal value.



Reason:

Board Trophies should matter as much as Main Trophies.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Roulette is the primary Casino mechanic.



Reason:

Every board requires a unique identity.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Wheel Of Death favors lower-Wealth players.



Reason:

Support comeback mechanics.

\## 2026-06-13



\### ACCEPTED



Special Room is the generic system name.



Casino board uses:



Special Room (VIP Room)



Reason:



Allows future boards to use the same system with different themes.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Wheel Of Death improves odds for losing players.



Wheel Of Death does not directly punish leading players.



Reason:



Comeback mechanics should help trailing players without making leaders feel punished for playing well.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Courier travel time should not exceed item production time.



Status:



Playtest Required.



Reason:



Production Buildings should not feel blocked for excessive periods.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Dice are a separate progression category.



Dice are not Items.



Reason:



Dice are permanent during a match and follow different acquisition and usage rules.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Dice do not use rarity tiers.



Reason:



Dice are strategic tools, not power progression.



\---



\### ACCEPTED



Players have unlimited Dice Collection.



No Dice Slots.



No Dice Loadouts.



Players select a Dice before movement.



Reason:



Encourage strategic choice and reduce unnecessary restrictions.
